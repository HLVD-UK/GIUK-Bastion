GIUK BASTION
NORTH ATLANTIC · WINTER 2031 · SEABED WARFARE DEMONSTRATOR

The situation. Tension with Russia is short of war but sharpening. The Northern Fleet has dispersed and three submarines are unaccounted for, moving to hold Euro-Atlantic energy pipelines and undersea data cables at risk — the seabed being the one front line nobody can see.

Your command. The Treasury has fixed your envelope; the fleet mix is yours. Exquisite crewed platforms — Type 26, Astute, P-8 — or the Hybrid Navy's autonomous sloops, XLUUVs and seabed sensor barriers. You cannot afford everything. Design the force, then fight with it.

Your mission. Hold the GIUK gap for 16 turns. Find and prosecute the submarines before they reach the cables and pipelines. Shadow the grey-zone vessels — you cannot engage them, only deter. Keep the lights on and the data flowing.

Each turn: issue movement orders, then END TURN. The enemy acts, and an intelligence brief follows — OSINT, media, and allied reporting that hints at what's coming without giving it away. Weigh it carefully. Not all of it is reliable.
